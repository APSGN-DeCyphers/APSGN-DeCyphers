ABOUT




The Digital Frontiers - Welcome to DeCyphers, where passion for technology unites us all!


















In the dynamic world of technology, the birth of DeCyphers, the tech club at Aster Public School, Greater Noida, in 2023 marked the dawn of a new era. Founded with a passion for all things tech, DeCyphers is a young and vibrant club on a mission to unite like-minded individuals, kindle the flames of innovation, and expand the horizons of technological knowledge among the students.

At the helm of this enterprising club are two visionary educators, Asima Chakrabarty and Priya Gupta, who recognised the need to create a platform where tech enthusiasts could come together, collaborate, and drive change through technology.

The club's mission is clear: to foster a sense of community among tech enthusiasts and to inspire innovation. DeCyphers aims to not only educate its members but also to extend its reach to the wider student body, spreading knowledge.

Currently comprising 11 dedicated members, DeCyphers represents a promising start. These members share a common goal—to explore the realms of technology and make a difference.

With visionary mentors, a dedicated group of members, and a clear mission, the club is poised to make a significant impact in the world of technology. As we strive to ignite innovation and inspire young minds, the future looks promising.
